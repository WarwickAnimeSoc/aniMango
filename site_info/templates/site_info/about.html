About

The Anime and Manga Society is Warwick's student-run society for anyone with an interest in Anime and Manga. The society is for anyone who would like to meet others who share these interests, or who just want to join us for one of the many events we run each academic year.

Our regular viewings take place in MS.05 (on the second floor of the Maths Building), starting at 18:30 every Tuesday during term time from Week 1 onwards. These events incorporate time for socialising as well as watching anime, so they're an excellent opportunity to meet others and have fun. In addition to this, we also hold many socials and other events across the year such as:

  • All-nighters — extended showings where we watch a selection of things that may not fit our usual format, with plenty of breaks for food and socialising.
  • Art/cosplay workshops — a chance to get help and advice with any projects you might be working on, or just generally chat with others about art or cosplay.
  • MCM trip — we usually go to the MCM London Comic Con during term 1.
  • Meals — socials where we enjoy the restaurants around Coventry.
We also have an extensive library of anime to watch and manga to read, available for our members to borrow.
